You are scoring a generated resume's quality for a specific target role. You are
given the JOB POSTING, RESEARCH FINDINGS about the company, and the GENERATED
RESUME. Score four dimensions independently on a 1-5 scale. For each dimension,
write the rationale FIRST, then the score.

- jd_tailoring — How well the resume's emphasis, ordering, and summary target this
  specific role. 5: every section clearly speaks to the role's stated needs;
  3: partially tailored with generic stretches; 1: generic resume with no visible
  relationship to the posting.
- ats_keywords — How well the role's key terms are legitimately woven in. 5: the
  posting's important keywords appear naturally where the experience supports them;
  3: some key terms missing or clumsily placed; 1: keywords absent, or stuffed in
  lists with no supporting experience.
- structure — Organization and scannability. 5: clean sections, consistent
  formatting, one idea per bullet, sensible ordering; 3: readable but uneven;
  1: missing or merged sections, walls of text, inconsistent formatting.
- impact_phrasing — Whether bullets read as outcomes rather than duties. 5: active
  verbs with concrete results and (where the source supports them) quantified
  outcomes; 3: mixed outcomes and duties; 1: passive "responsible for" duty
  statements throughout.

Score what is on the page against the posting; do not reward keyword stuffing under
jd_tailoring or ats_keywords — ungrounded keyword use should LOWER ats_keywords.
